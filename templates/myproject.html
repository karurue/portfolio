EDONATION KENYA
E DONATION is a platform in which a child in school can get sponsorship to be able to continue with his/her dreams. As a child we all had ambitions in life this may be to either be a doctor, engineer, pilot or a lawyer. This platform is used to enable a child be able to see his/her dreams come true.Through your support we will get a child to go through his/her studies and graduate successfully.Donations come from the alumni of a particular school, an organization, agencies , parents of the school or even well wishers.It is our great desire to see you support us support our children.

MISSING PERSONS KENYA
This is one project that is close to my heart. We have all heard of the events of children getting lost never to be found for days,months or even years. This saddens many parents as most of them dont get the assistance even after a long period of searching and reporting. Of late we have also seen these messages in various platforms such as whatsapp or even twitter and this has enabled the children get located and happily reunited with their parents.To get this going we need a website in Kenya that deals with such cases to give hope to such families and show them there is something that can be done. Most people think that it is only the parents who lose their children but the truth is that children also lose their parents and stay for years looking for them.To assist curb this or easen the search this website is going to collaborate with the authorities and families and the public in general to help in the search
